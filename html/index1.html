About

As we enter a new and exciting automated age for the art industry, e Art Gallery is an initiative that fuses both tradition and modernity to create a unique online platform promoting Indian Art domestically and across the globe.

Supporting Arts & Culture around the World

We believe technology opens doors, and we created the app as a part of our mission to make art and culture accessible to all -– in-person, at-home, anywhere.

Easy to use for visitors

This format doesn’t have any physical place and is open at any time, regardless of timezone. People don’t have to get to the exhibit from all over the world.

Affordability

The paintings are showcased at online art galleries are less expensive and the paintings are been delivered at the doorstep. If you visit any physical art gallery, you may get most of the paintings which will not suit your pocket or over budget.
